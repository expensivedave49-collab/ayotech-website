FAQ

Answers to Common Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Chat with us on WhatsApp — we respond within the hour.

💬 Ask on WhatsApp
How long does it take to build a website? +
It depends on the project scope. A simple business website takes 1–2 weeks, a web application or custom platform takes 4–12 weeks, and large enterprise systems can take 3–6 months. We give clear timelines during the discovery phase.
How much does a project cost? +
Costs vary based on complexity and features. A basic website starts from ₦150,000. Mobile apps and web applications range from ₦500,000 to ₦5M+. We offer transparent, itemized quotes — no hidden fees. Contact us for a free estimate.
Do you work with clients outside Nigeria? +
Absolutely. We work with clients across Africa, the UK, USA, Canada, and beyond. Our communication tools and processes are built for remote collaboration — video calls, project management tools, and async updates keep everyone aligned.
What happens after the project is launched? +
We provide 30 days of free post-launch support for bug fixes and adjustments. Beyond that, we offer affordable maintenance packages for hosting, updates, security patches, and feature additions.
Can you take over a project another developer started? +
Yes. We regularly rescue and take over projects from other developers. We'll audit the existing codebase, give you an honest assessment, and propose the best path forward — whether that's refactoring, rebuilding, or continuing where it was left off.
Do you sign NDAs and contracts? +
Yes, always. Every project begins with a signed contract that covers scope, timeline, payment terms, IP ownership, and confidentiality. Your ideas and business information are always protected with us.
